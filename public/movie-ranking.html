My Top 3 Favourite Movies of all time

Rush Hour (1, 2 & 3)

Best and funniest duo between Chris Tucker (Carter) and Jackie Chan (Lee). Both make me laugh so much. Alongside action was entertaining.

Avengers Infinity War

Very exciting from beginning, middle and end. Also surprising with the outcome of the movie, which I did not expected at all.

Den of Thieves

I am a fan of shootout heist movies. This movie brought in my opinion a lot of impactful moments that I was so happy to see. Plus, there is Den of Thieves 2 coming out very soon.
